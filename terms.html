2. Platform Description

BloodLink Pro is a free, non-commercial blood donor-matching platform built for educational and humanitarian purposes. The platform:

  • Connects blood donors with patients in need
  • Provides a searchable database of registered blood donors
  • Facilitates emergency blood request notifications
  • Lists blood banks and their contact information
  • Provides blood compatibility reference information
BloodLink Pro does NOT provide medical services, guarantee blood availability, arrange blood transfusions, or employ medical professionals. We are a technology platform only.

3. Eligibility

To use BloodLink Pro, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Be a resident of India
  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Have a valid mobile number and email address

To register as a blood donor, you must additionally:

  • Be between 18–65 years of age
  • Weigh at least 45 kg
  • Be in good health at the time of donation
  • Not have donated blood in the last 56 days (whole blood)
  • Meet all medical eligibility criteria as per Indian blood donation guidelines

5. Donor Registration Rules

By registering as a blood donor, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate information about your blood group, health status, and availability
  • Update your availability status promptly when it changes
  • Respond to genuine blood requests in good faith
  • Not register with false information or someone else's details
  • Understand that your contact information will be visible to users searching for blood
  • Consult a doctor before donating if you have any health concerns
Fake Entries: Registering with false information, fake phone numbers, or impersonating another person is strictly prohibited and may result in permanent account ban and legal action.

All donor registrations are subject to admin verification before appearing in search results.

6. Emergency Request Rules

The emergency blood request feature is for genuine medical emergencies only. By submitting an emergency request, you agree that:

  • The request is for a real, genuine medical emergency
  • You will not submit fake or test emergency requests
  • You are limited to 3 emergency requests per day per device
  • You will also contact your nearest hospital and call 108 simultaneously
  • BloodLink Pro is not a substitute for professional medical emergency services

Misuse of the emergency system (fake requests, spam) will result in immediate account termination.
