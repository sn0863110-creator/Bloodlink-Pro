Summary: BloodLink Pro is a free blood donor-matching platform. We collect only the minimum data needed to connect donors with patients. We never sell your data. Donor contact details are visible to users searching for blood — this is the core purpose of the platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you use BloodLink Pro:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password (hashed), mobile number, city
  • Donor Profile: Blood group, availability status, last donation date, health status
  • Emergency Requests: Patient name, blood group needed, hospital/location, contact number, urgency level
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates (only when you grant permission) — used to show nearest donors
  • Device Information: Browser type, screen resolution, timezone — used for analytics and device fingerprinting to prevent spam

We do not collect: Aadhaar number, PAN card, financial information, or any government ID.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate the BloodLink Pro platform:

  • Display your donor profile in search results so patients can find you
  • Send emergency blood request notifications to matching donors
  • Verify your account and prevent duplicate or fake registrations
  • Show you relevant donors based on your city and blood group search
  • Admin verification of donor profiles before they appear publicly
  • Improve platform performance and fix bugs
  • Send important service updates (not marketing emails)
Important: When you register as a donor, your name, blood group, city, and phone number will be visible to all users searching for blood donors. This is the core function of the platform. If you do not want your information visible, please do not register as a donor, or toggle your availability to "Not Available."

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

We may share your information only in these limited cases:

  • With other users: Donor profiles (name, blood group, city, phone) are visible to registered users searching for blood
  • Emergency notifications: Your contact details may be shared with patients submitting emergency blood requests
  • Service providers: We use JSONBin (data storage), EmailJS (email notifications), and Firebase (authentication) — all under their respective privacy policies
  • Legal requirements: If required by Indian law or court order

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using the following services:

  • JSONBin.io: Donor and user data stored in encrypted JSON bins
  • Firebase Firestore: (Future) Cloud database with Google-grade security
  • Browser localStorage: Session data, preferences (dark mode, language) stored locally on your device

We implement reasonable security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Password hashing (never stored in plain text)
  • Admin-only access to sensitive operations
  • Rate limiting on emergency requests to prevent abuse
  • CAPTCHA verification on emergency forms

However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correction: Update your profile information via your account settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and donor profile by emailing us
  • Availability Toggle: Set yourself as "Not Available" to hide from search results without deleting your account
  • Opt-out: You can stop receiving notifications by contacting us

To exercise any of these rights, email us at sn0863110@gmail.com

6. Cookies & Local Storage

BloodLink Pro uses browser localStorage (not traditional cookies) to store:

  • Your login session (user profile data)
  • Dark mode preference
  • Language preference (Hindi/English)
  • Cached donor data for offline use (PWA)
  • Visitor count (anonymous, device-fingerprint based)
  • Emergency request rate limit counter

You can clear this data anytime by clearing your browser's site data. This will log you out.

7. Third-Party Services

BloodLink Pro integrates with these third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: Font loading — Google Privacy Policy
  • JSONBin.io: Data storage — JSONBin Privacy Policy
  • EmailJS: Email notifications — EmailJS Privacy Policy
  • Firebase (Google): Authentication & database — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • OpenStreetMap / Leaflet: Maps — OSM Privacy Policy
  • QR Server API: QR code generation — no personal data sent

8. Children's Privacy

BloodLink Pro is not intended for users under 18 years of age. Blood donation requires donors to be at least 18 years old as per Indian medical guidelines.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Show a notification on the website

Continued use of BloodLink Pro after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
